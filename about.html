About Bloom

We are a Brighton-based company focused on the business of running aesthetic, wellness and beauty clinics, through research, publishing and the technology we build for the sector.

Why we exist

Aesthetic, wellness and beauty clinic owners are entrepreneurs. They have built skilled businesses from the ground up, and they make complex commercial decisions every day alongside the clinical work.

Most coverage of this sector focuses on treatments, consumer trends and patient outcomes. The strategic and operational decisions that go into building these businesses get far less attention. That is the gap Bloom exists to fill.

We believe the best technology for any sector comes from genuinely understanding how the businesses in it are built and run, not from assuming you already know.

How we work

We speak directly with clinic owners and managers about the business side of their work: how they structure their teams, how they approach growth, how they think about the tools and systems they use. We publish that research as features and articles, and it forms the foundation for the technology we develop.

We focus exclusively on aesthetic, wellness and beauty clinics, which means we develop real familiarity with how these businesses operate rather than working across many different sectors at once.

Want to be involved?

If you run an aesthetic, wellness or beauty clinic and would be open to sharing your perspective on the business side of what you do, we would genuinely love to hear from you.
